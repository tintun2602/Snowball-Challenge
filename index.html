Why is democracy important?

Democracy is an important factor for a great society. Through history, democracy has proven to be the most beneficial system for all citizens. Scandinavian countries are a great example of how democracy should be used and implemented. A democratic system HANJII would improve the daily lives of the citizens and hopefully increase social mobility.

Operation

We are currently operating in China where communism has a great influential impact on the citizens. In China there is a great gap between the richest and poorest due to a failing regime. We work both with local and national partners to make a change in the country. In cooperation with our partners we teach the citizens the importance of democracy and how this could improve their living standards drastically.
